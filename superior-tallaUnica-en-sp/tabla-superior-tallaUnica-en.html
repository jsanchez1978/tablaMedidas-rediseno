Find your size

Find the perfect size by following these steps. When measuring yourself, wear a comfy bra without padding for an accurate measurement. Once you have your measurements, use the size calculator to figure out what will fit you.

How to measure

Step 1: Band
Measure your band size by placing your measuring tape right under your arms and over the bust

Step 2: Bust
Measure the contour of your bust in inches by placing a measuring tape over the fullest part.
